Oversight Logo Design.

Categories: Graphic Design

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator

This was a logo design project for Oversight, an company focused on improving the Apex Legends esports scene by hosting tournaments & pro scrims.

Process:

The process of this logo design went very quickly, running from start to finish in just a couple days. I had the name and color scheme they wanted and the freedom to test out whatever ideas I felt could represent them. With the name 'Oversight' representing their role in overseeing and supporting the Apex Legends pro community in whatever ways they could, including running a tournament series and pro scrims, my ideation started in the direction of an eye shape, while trying to incorporate the letters 'O' and 'S'. I also experimented with a crosshair design within the eye to represent their involvement in the first-person shooter genre. When showing some of the concepts, they liked the idea of the crosshair but decided to move past the eye visuals. Through multiple iterations, bringing back the idea of a combined 'O' and 'S' shape in the middle, while setting the crosshair as the border, we settled on a final design.

Reflections:

I'm generally satisfied with how this logo turned out. While I really enjoyed some of the earlier concepts I came up with, I was able to adapt to the client's vision to create something everyone was happy with.
