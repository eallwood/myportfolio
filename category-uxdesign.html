UX/UI Design.

Throughout all my time in creative work, I have found a special interest in UX/UI design. The combination of both creative decision-making and user-based problem solving appeals to me and I have been taking courses to improve my UX skills.

Smart Materials Exhibit Microsite Design.

Created as a course project in IAT235 (Information Design) this website design was built to represent an art exhibit that explored the creative use of 'smart materials'. By enforcing the ideas of experimentation and exploration found in the exhibit, this website uses the visual metaphor of a tabletop in which all content is quickly reachable.
