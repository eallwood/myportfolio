Apex Legends Player Intros & Character Recolors.

Categories: 3d Art Animation

Tools Used: Photoshop (editing character textures), Blender (rendering character animations), Adobe After Effects (creating text animations), Adobe Premiere Pro (final video editing)

While working on the Oversight x Hyperluxe Apex Legends Circuit as creative lead, one of my first projects was leading the creation of hype videos for the top 2 teams going into playoffs, NRG and Team Liquid. While helping guide the video editors creating these videos, I also stepped in to create more complex segments like player intros. This project shows the individual player intros as well as the final result from the combined efforts of me and the video editors.

NRG Team Highlight Video

Team Liquid Highlight Video

Reflections:

Having limited time and very little experience working with the existing character models, I did not feel like these represented the best of what I could do. Being more comfortable in Cinema4D compared to Blender as well, with the character models only working with Blender, a lot of my time went into adjusting my workflow and adapting to the new software which left little time for improvement. With the experience I have now, if I were to repeat a similar project, I would love to improve on the background, building out a proper scene rather than a flat color, and I would also put more time into better text animations, to match the fast paced action of the in-game clips.
